# Research Notes

## Similar Games

- **A Normal Lost Phone**: useful reference for intimate, cross-app inference where details in one app unlock another. Source: https://dearvillagers.itch.io/a-normal-lost-phone
- **Sara Is Missing**: useful reference for missing-person phone horror, protected files, hidden messages, and recovered/decrypted data. Source: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/sara-is-missing/id1181038779
- **SIMULACRA**: useful reference for corrupted data, strange phone behavior, messaging, email, gallery fragments, and retracing final steps through a simulated phone. Source: https://store.steampowered.com/app/712730/SIMULACRA/
- **Duskwood**: useful reference for detective chat fiction, multimedia evidence, voicemails, choices, and puzzle variety. Source: https://everbytestudio.com/Press/sheet.php?p=duskwood
- **Peek a Phone**: useful reference for a case-based library of realistic phone mysteries. Source: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.peekaphone.app

## Creepy Case Inspiration

- **Numbers stations**: shortwave broadcasts associated with encoded messages; adapted as `Dead Air at 03:17`, using transcripts, frequencies, and scheduled notifications. Source: https://priyom.org/number-stations
- **Vanishing hitchhiker folklore**: a classic recurring urban legend; adapted as `Passenger Zero`, using rideshare receipts and GPS anomalies rather than copying any specific story. Source: https://www.snopes.com/fact-check/the-vanishing-hitchhiker/
- **Dark Watchers folklore**: shadowy figures seen on ridges; adapted as `The Ridge Watchers`, focusing on EXIF sorting, compass bearings, and dusk photos. Source: https://legendsofwindemere.com/2024/10/09/the-dark-watchers-of-california/
- **Liminal-space horror / Backrooms-style digital folklore**: unsettling impossible architecture; adapted as `Exit Past Closing`, using mall maps, camera-loop mismatches, and maintenance codes. Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Backrooms

## Design Gimmicks Adapted

- Case library with difficulty, solved counts, time estimates, and mechanic previews.
- Active leads on the emulated phone home screen, so the player has diegetic next steps.
- Evidence is earned by solving small puzzles rather than pressing a save button.
- Photo metadata becomes evidence after the player unlocks hidden images.
- Theory lab lets players test suspects and see evidence contradictions before submitting the final deduction.
